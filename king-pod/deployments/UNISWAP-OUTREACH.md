# Uniswap outreach — who to PM (Base)

**Truth first:** Creating an eUSD/USDC or ELE/USDC pool on Uniswap **does not need a Uniswap PM**.  
Factory is permissionless. What you need is **USDC to seed** (Door 3 buyer / wire / PSM reserve).

Uniswap Labs contacts help with **visibility / routing / co-marketing** after (or while) you seed — not with minting dollars.
